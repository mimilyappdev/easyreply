## HOW TO USE

EasyReply runs in your browser — no installation required.

Just open the HTML file in your browser and you're ready to go.

All data is stored locally on your device
(browser localStorage).

Data is saved per device and does not sync automatically.

■ Getting Started

Open "EasyReply_EN.html" in your browser
(Chrome / Safari / Edge, etc.).

You can add it to your home screen to use it like a native app.

iPhone:
Open in Safari → Share → Add to Home Screen

Android:
Browser menu → Install App
or Add to Home Screen

■ Important: About Data Storage

App data is stored in your browser's localStorage.

Data may be lost in the following situations:

- Clearing browser cache or site data
- Removing or reinstalling the home screen shortcut
- Changing devices or resetting your device

It is strongly recommended to regularly back up your data
via Settings → Export (JSON).

■ Basic Usage

1. Select a Phase

Choose from the tabs at the top:
"Inquiry", "In Progress", or "Completed".

2. Select a Reply Pattern

Tap a chip ("Accept", "Decline", etc.)
that fits your situation.

3. Build your reply with buttons

Tap a button in the left panel to preview the text
on the right.

Select multiple buttons to combine them.

4. Edit the preview (optional)

The preview area is directly editable.
Add a name or adjust the wording before sending.

5. Copy and use

Tap "Copy" to copy the text to your clipboard.

6. Attach a download link (optional)

Tap "DL Link" to enter a download URL and password.
They will be automatically appended to the preview.

7. Back up your data

Go to Settings (⚙) → Export to save a JSON backup file.
When switching devices, use Import to restore your data.

■ Theme Colors

Go to Settings (⚙) → Theme Color to switch between
Default, Dark, and My Color (2 custom slots).

My Color lets you customize 5 color seeds freely.

■ Supported Environments

Latest versions of major modern browsers recommended:
Chrome / Safari / Edge.

Firefox and some specialized browsers may not
function correctly.

■ Updates

If bug fixes or improvements are made, you can download
the latest version from your Booth / Gumroad download page.

However, free updates for future feature additions,
spec changes, or new products are not guaranteed.

## MIGRATING FROM THE FREE VERSION

1. In the free version, go to Settings → Export
   to back up your data.

2. Open the downloaded EasyReply_EN.html in your browser.

3. If you had the free version added to your home screen,
   remove it and add the new version instead.

4. Go to Settings → Import to restore your backup.
